Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Pioneering groundbreaking solutions to drive growth and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Building a resilient future through eco-friendly and responsible practices.

Infrastructure

Developing world-class facilities to support connectivity and economic progress.

Empowerment

Uplifting communities with education, equity, and economic opportunities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh stands at a historic crossroads, with the opportunity to redefine its future through innovation, inclusivity, and sustainable development. Our nation’s strength lies in its people—their resilience, creativity, and unyielding spirit. Together, we must work to eradicate poverty, ensure social justice, and empower every citizen to contribute meaningfully to our shared prosperity. By embracing collaboration and forward-thinking solutions, we can transform Bangladesh into a model of equitable growth and environmental stewardship, inspiring nations worldwide to follow our example."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
